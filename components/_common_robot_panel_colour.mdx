### Custom Robot Panel Colour

Your robot's yellow panels can be swapped for a custom colour.
We use TIGER Drylac powder coating on these parts.
TIGER has a wide range of colours, though some are special order.

Talk to a Clearpath Robotics salesperson to discuss custom colour prices and lead times: [research-sales@clearpathrobotics.com](mailto:research-sales@clearpathrobotics.com)
