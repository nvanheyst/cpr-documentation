:::note Technical Specification Footnotes

_<sup>1</sup> &ensp; Continuous operation on relatively flat terrain with wide turns. See also [Important System Limits](#important-system-limits)_ <br />

_<sup>2</sup> &ensp; Robot climbing 30° grade with high-mounted payload, or turning in place in high-friction conditions such as asphalt, concrete, and carpet. See also [Important System Limits](#important-system-limits)_ <br />

_<sup>3</sup> &ensp; Maximum speed should be limited to 1 m/s in off-road conditions when the payload exceeds 50 kg. See also [Important System Limits](#important-system-limits)_ <br />

_<sup>4</sup> &ensp; See also [Important System Limits](#important-system-limits) for additional considerations related to slopes._ <br />

_<sup>5</sup> &ensp; Robot must be powered on at a minimum temperature of 0 °C._ <br />
_You may notice odd sensor data as the AMP or Observer warms up; such as with the included [Luxonis OAK-D Pro](https://docs.luxonis.com/hardware/platform/environmental-specifications/operating-temperature/#Operating%20temperature-Min%20temperature) cameras which limits its functionality when internal components are cold._ <br />

_<sup>6</sup> &ensp; Based on 33% driving at 2 m/s with 50 kg payload on level ground and 67% idle._ <br />

_<sup>7</sup> &ensp; With reference to the Husky A300's `base_link`._ <br />
