### Confirming That OutdoorNav Software Is Functioning

Husky AMP includes OutdoorNav Software, which allows the robot to be operated autonomously.
Follow the steps below to confirm that OutdoorNav Software is functioning.

1. [Connect to Network and Web UI access](/docs_outdoornav_user_manual/getting_started#networking_setup_and_ui_access)
1. [Connect to an RTK Source](/docs_outdoornav_user_manual/getting_started#rtk_setup)
1. [Check camera views](/docs_outdoornav_user_manual/getting_started#checking-camera-feeds)
1. Complete system configuration:
   1. [Load map tiles](/docs_outdoornav_user_manual/getting_started#loading_map_tiles)
   1. [Set dock location](/docs_outdoornav_user_manual/getting_started#set-dock-location), if a dock is included with your system
1. [Create your first map](/docs_outdoornav_user_manual/getting_started#create-your-first-map)
1. [Create your first mission](/docs_outdoornav_user_manual/getting_started#create-your-first-mission)
1. [Execute your first mission](/docs_outdoornav_user_manual/getting_started#execute-your-first-mission)
