### Fan Expansion {#fan-expansion}

The System Interface circuit board has support for 4 additional 12 V brushless 4-wire PWM fans.
Ports FAN 1, FAN 2, FAN 3, and FAN 4 are already used by the base Husky A300.
Ports FAN 5, FAN 6, FAN 7, and FAN 8 are available for your fan expansions.

:::info Examples Of Supported fans

* [CFM-8025BG-170-517-22](https://www.digikey.ca/en/products/detail/cui-devices/CFM-8025BG-170-517-22/16581761)
* [9GA0812P4J001](https://www.digikey.ca/en/products/detail/sanyo-denki-america-inc/9GA0812P4J001/6192305).

:::

The pinout for each fan is:

| Pin | Function                       |
| :-- | :----------------------------- |
| 1   | 12 V                           |
| 2   | GND                            |
| 3   | Tachometer _(output from fan)_ |
| 4   | PWM _(input to fan, 3.3 V)_    |

The speed of each fan can be controlled by publishing to the `/a300_#####/platform/mcu/cmd_fans` ROS topic.

The speed of each fan can be monitored by reading the `/a300_#####/platform/mcu/status/fans` ROS topic.

In both cases, the fan value is in the range from 0 _(not spinning)_, to 255 _(max speed)_.

:::info

Fans require spool up time on startup.
Setting a fan to a very low value may result in the fan not rotating.
You should experiment to find minimum PWM value that causes your fan to spin.

:::
