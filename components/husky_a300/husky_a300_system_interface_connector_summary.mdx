The System Interface Assembly is a circuit board that includes power supplies, lighting controllers, motor controllers, and the Husky's microcontroller.
This circuit board has connectors for: power, fans, relays, GPIOs, auxiliary inputs, auxiliary outputs, and Emergency Stop devices.
