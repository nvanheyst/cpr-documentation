To remove Husky's _Top Plate_:

1.  Turn off the Husky.
2.  Open the _Rear Charge Port Door_.
3.  Flip the _Circuit Breaker_ to the Off position.
4.  Close the _Rear Charge Port Door_.
5.  Remove the seven M6×1 socket head screws from the _Top Plate_.
    Note that one of the screws also has a sealing washer attached to it.
6.  If applicable, remove any of your custom cables from the _Top Plate's_ connector openings. 
7.  Lift the _Top Plate_ off of the robot.

:::note

When reinstalling the _Top Plate_:

* Torque the tray's 4 screws to 3 N·m.

:::

:::danger

Do not drag the _Top Plate_ on the robot, because it may damage the bulb seal around the Husky's chassis.

:::
