### base_link

`base_link` is the primary coordinate system for the robot.
All sensors, manipulators, and brackets are positioned with reference to `base link`.
[This ROS 2 Documentation page](https://docs.ros.org/en/jazzy/Tutorials/Intermediate/URDF/Building-a-Visual-Robot-Model-with-URDF-from-Scratch.html) explains how a robot's model is constructed.

Husky's model is included on the robot, and is available on our public GitHub.
Husky's `base_link` is located on the bottom of the chassis, with +X pointed toward the front panel, and +Y toward the robot's left side.
