### Removing The Enclosure

[//]: <> (TODO, add an image of the enclosure being removed.)

<details>
  <summary>To remove the large electronics enclosure:</summary>

  1.  Turn off the Husky.
  2.  Open the _Rear Charge Port Door_.
  3.  Flip the _Circuit Breaker_ to the Off position.
  4.  Close the _Rear Charge Port Door_.
  5.  Remove the _Side Panels_.
  6.  Disconnect all the cables from the Base Robot &#8594; AMP Enclosure _Connector Bulkhead_.
  7.  Remove the seven M6×1 socket head screws that attach the _Enclosure_ to the base robot.
      
      :::note

      The seventh screw is near the rear-left corner of the robot.

      :::

  8.  Lift the _Enclosure_ off the base robot.

  <Admonition type="danger" title=" ">
    Do not drag the _Enclosure_ on the robot, because it may damage the bulb seal around the Husky's chassis.
  </Admonition>

  :::note

  When reinstalling the _Enclosure_:

  * Torque the 7 screws to 5 N·m.
  * Torque the _Side Panel's_ nuts to 3 N·m.

  :::

</details>
