The lifting equipment and straps must be rated for the weight of Husky and any mounted payloads, plus any factor of safety per your local worksite regulations.
Refer to the [Husky's Technical Specifications](#technical-specifications) for the weight of Husky and its maximum payload.

:::note

There are metal bosses on the bottom of Husky to help align lifting straps.

:::

:::info

Keep the rigging's lifting hook at least 1 metre above the Husky's top surface.
This makes a steep sling angle, which reduces the lateral loads on the Husky's chassis.

:::

:::safety-warning

Never lift a Husky over a person.

:::
