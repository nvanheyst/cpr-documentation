### Network Switch

<center>
  <img
    src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_network_switch_1.png"
    width="700"
  />
</center>

The network switch connects the primary computer, the rear debugging port, and any additional Ethernet devices.

<details>
  <summary>To remove the switch:</summary>
  
  1.  Shut off the robot's circuit breaker.
  2.  Remove Husky's _Top Plate_.
  3.  Remove any network cables from the switch.
  4.  Remove Husky's _Front Panel_.
  5.  Remove the 4 screws and waterproofing washers, while holding onto the switch.
  6.  Disconnect the switch's power cable, and remove it from the robot.

<center>
  <img
    src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_network_switch_3.png"
    width="500"
  />
</center>

  :::note

    When reinstalling the switch:

    * Make sure the power cable is connected.
    * Make sure the waterproofing washers are included.
    * Torque the 4 screws to 1.2 N·m.

  :::

</details>

<details>
  <summary>To remove the switch from its subassembly brackets:</summary>

  <center>
    <img
      src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_network_switch_2.png"
      width="500"
    />
  </center>
  
  1.  Remove the 4 flat head screws.
  2.  Remove the switch from the plastic brackets.

  :::note

    When reinstalling the switch:

    * Torque the 4 screws to 0.5 N·m.

  :::

</details>
