**Always perform a risk assessment prior to any custom integrations.**

Custom integrations are outside the scope of the base robot's safety
assessment and custom integrations may result in new hazards.

To attach custom hardware to Husky, you will have to take care of mechanical mounting, electrical
supply, and software integration. This guide aims to equip you with respect to these challenges.
